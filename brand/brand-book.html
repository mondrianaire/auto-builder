A vintage aviation / mission-control documentation aesthetic. Warm cream paper, four-color faded rainbow stripe, italic Georgia serif headlines, monospace data, italic Georgia body. The discipline of a flight manifest, not the cheerfulness of a dashboard.

01Identity

OttoBLD is the rebrand of AutoBuilder — a build-automation orchestration system. The "Codex" is its canonical registry of every build the system has produced. The wordmark is a single token, mixed case: OttoBLD.

What it is: warm cream, vintage paper documentation, faded rainbow as identity mark, italic Georgia + monospace + system sans.
What it is not: industrial brutalism, cool grey tech, PlayStation primaries, flat SaaS dashboard, holographic, vaporwave.

09Anti-patterns · explicitly avoid

These were explored and rejected during the rebrand exploration. Do not return to them.
✕ Industrial brutalism (Otto thin + BLD heavy, slate-and-orange) — too cold for this brand.
✕ Cool grey base (#A6A6A6 or PS1 grey) — too tech, loses the document warmth.
✕ PlayStation-saturated primaries (#e60012 red, #ffce00 yellow, #006fcd blue) — too modern arcade.
✕ Pure black or pure white backgrounds — breaks the paper feel.
✕ Italic ALL CAPS headers mixed with lowercase mono slugs in the same row — case mismatch.
✕ Clinical SaaS dashboard layouts (rounded cards, drop shadows everywhere) — wrong vocabulary.
✕ Hot-pink / cyan vaporwave palette — wrong era.
✕ Holographic / glass / iridescent surfaces — wrong material.
